---
name: plan
description: Create short outcome-oriented plans for complex, ambiguous, or risky tasks
---

- Use only for complex, ambiguous, or risky tasks — not simple work.
- Make a short plan with 3 to 7 outcome-oriented steps.
- Do not over-plan simple tasks.
- Update the plan if new findings change execution.
- Move from planning to implementation quickly.
- Every step must have a clear success criterion.
